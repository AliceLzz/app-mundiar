    Transforming education through technology. This is our goal! Our mission is to contribute to education - which is a big challenge in Brazil - a country that is among the last 5 in the International Student Assessment Program (Pisa) carried out in 2018. And in this scenario, teachers find it difficult to propose Extra and interactive content for students and teaching materials become tiring and unattractive in the midst of so much technology that young people are already involved with. In this scenario, MundiAR appears, a platform with the objective of transforming learning using interactivity, gamification and augmented reality, connecting this content with schools, teachers and students.
